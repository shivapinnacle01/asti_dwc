Award in Assessing Vocational Related Achievement

The Level 3 Award in Assessing Vocationally Related Achievement is designed for professionals who evaluate both knowledge and skills in vocational fields. They utilize a range of assessment methods including simulations, skills tests, oral and written questioning, assignments, projects, case studies, and Recognition of Prior Learning (RPL). These assessments occur in various settings such as training workshops, classrooms, or other learning environments.

This qualification aims to enhance the skills and knowledge of professionals who assess occupational competence in work settings as well as vocational skills, knowledge, and understanding in non-work environments like workshops, classrooms, or training settings.

Course Overview

The Level 3 Certificate in Assessing Vocational Achievement course is ideal for those who assess vocational skills, knowledge, and understanding in settings outside of the workplace, such as workshops, classrooms, or training environments.

Throughout the course, you'll acquire the necessary knowledge and skills to effectively plan assessments, choose appropriate assessment methods, conduct assessments, provide feedback to learners, contribute to quality assurance, and maintain accurate assessment records. This NVQ Assessors course is tailored for individuals tasked with assessing competence in either workplace or classroom settings.

Course Objective

This qualification caters to individuals aged 19 and above, with no specific prerequisites. However, having previously attained a Level 2 qualification might be beneficial. It's up to the centers to ensure that this qualification suits the learners' age and capabilities.

The aim is to make this qualification easily accessible without unnecessary hurdles. Entry will involve a center interview, and assessment will be tailored to each individual.

In some cases, individuals with significant experience but lacking formal qualifications may be considered, pending an interview and demonstration of their ability to handle the program requirements.

This qualification covers the fundamental principles necessary for assessment staff, while also including additional units that outline proficient practice. This approach offers flexibility and better caters to the needs of individuals aiming to become assessors.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is a Level 3 vocational qualification?

A Level 3 vocational qualification is an intermediate-level certification that demonstrates proficiency in a specific business or profession. It equips individuals with the practical skills and knowledge required for various vocational roles and is equivalent to an A-level qualification.

Is Level 3 higher than a diploma?

Yes, Level 3 is typically higher than a diploma. A Level 3 qualification indicates a higher level of skill and knowledge mastery compared to a standard diploma, which is often at Level 2 or lower.

How long does it take to get a Level 3 certificate?

The duration to obtain a Level 3 certificate varies depending on the specific course and individual learning pace from 6 months to 1 year. Generally, it can take anywhere from several months to a year to complete a Level 3 certificate program.
