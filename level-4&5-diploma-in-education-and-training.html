Career Pathways in Education and Training

You will be ready to pursue a profession as an adult education teacher with the help of ASTI's Education and Training Diploma program. A wide range of educational environments, such as adult education, work-based learning, further education, and community learning, will employ you.

Education administrator

The education administrator is in charge of the day-to-day operations at a school, including scheduling, budgeting, disciplinary measures, and event coordination.

Teaching

The most well-known professional path for those with a degree in education is undoubtedly teaching, which is a fantastic choice if you enjoy working with adults or children in a classroom environment.

Counselors in schools

School counselors support students in their social, academic, and emotional development as well as in navigating the transition from school to college or the workforce. As a school counselor, you will have a significant impact on kids' growth and assist them in realizing their greatest potential outside of the classroom.

Preschool or kindergarten Teacher

Planning, carrying out, and evaluating lessons are all parts of teaching in kindergarten. You’ll be patient and inventive when instructing kids. The responsibilities of a kindergarten teacher include creating a lesson plan and motivating the students through games and other teaching techniques.
